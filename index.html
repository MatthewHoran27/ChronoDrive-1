Movie Description

In the year 3045, humanity stands on the brink of technological transcendence, and at the forefront of this new age is Dr. Paul Jacobs — a brilliant but impulsive physicist obsessed with unlocking the secrets of time itself. After years of grueling experiments, personal sacrifice, and pushing the boundaries of scientific ethics, Jacobs finally succeeds: he invents the world’s first functional time travel device. Driven by ambition and an insatiable thirst for discovery, he becomes the first human to test it.
But something goes horribly wrong.
Instead of traveling freely through time, Jacobs becomes trapped within a chaotic time loop, reliving the same critical moments of his experiment over and over, each cycle pulling him further from reality and closer to madness. His disappearance and the strange anomalies emanating from the lab send shockwaves through the scientific community.
Enter Dr. Ahmed Habib, Jacobs' trusted lab assistant and closest confidant — a brilliant mind in his own right, but far more cautious. Realizing that time itself is beginning to unravel around the lab, Habib must navigate a collapsing timestream, decipher Jacobs’ fragmented research, and confront his own fears about tampering with forces beyond comprehension. Racing against the clock, Habib embarks on a desperate mission to rescue his mentor, facing paradoxes, alternate timelines, and a revelation that threatens to not only consume them both — but rewrite history itself.
Chrono Drift is a thrilling, high-concept sci-fi epic exploring ambition, sacrifice, and the unstoppable consequences of human innovation.
